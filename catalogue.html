Lot 01 · Kirinyaga · 1,900m

Summit Reserve

Our most coveted lot, grown at 1,900m on the volcanic slopes of Mount Kenya. The high altitude and slow cherry maturation create extraordinary complexity — blackcurrant and bergamot notes lead into a luminous, lingering finish that defines what Kenya AA can be at its absolute peak.

Lot 02 · Nyeri · 1,850m

Midnight Bloom

From Nyeri's legendary red volcanic soils — soil so rich it's been called the most prized growing earth in Kenya. Midnight Bloom carries a wine-dark body, dark cherry intensity, damask rose aromatics and a bittersweet cocoa finish. A lot for those who want depth and drama in every cup.

Lot 03 · Murang'a · 1,800m

Golden Mist

Named for the soft morning mist that blankets Murang'a's southern slopes. The gentlest of our three lots — honeyed sweetness, ripe stone fruit, and a silky, gentle finish make Golden Mist the most approachable expression of Kenya AB, perfect for blends or as a standout single-origin pour.
