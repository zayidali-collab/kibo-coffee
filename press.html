Brand Overview

The Kibo Story

Kibo Coffee was founded in Nairobi in 2025 with a single conviction — that Kenya's highland coffee is the finest in the world, and that it deserves to be positioned and marketed as such.

The brand takes its name from Kibo, the highest peak of Kilimanjaro — Africa's roof. It represents altitude, excellence and the belief that the best things in life are found at the top.

Kibo sources exclusively from three counties — Kirinyaga, Nyeri and Murang'a — the most celebrated coffee-growing regions in Kenya. Every lot is single-origin, hand-picked, and traceable to the cooperative that grew it.

Talking Points

Why Kibo Matters

Origin Story
Africa's Most Undervalued Export
Kenya consistently produces some of the world's highest-scoring specialty coffee, yet it is exported as a commodity at a fraction of its true market value. Kibo was built to change that.
Market Opportunity
The Gulf's Growing Specialty Market
Premium coffee consumption in the UAE, Saudi Arabia and Qatar is growing at 15%+ annually. Kibo is positioned to be the defining Kenyan voice in that market.
Quality Story
Kenya AA — The Rarest Grade
Kenya AA (screen size 18+) represents less than 12% of Kenya's total coffee production. Kibo sources exclusively at this grade — making every lot genuinely rare and premium.
