Our Story

Born in the
Highlands.

Kenya AA Grade✦Kirinyaga · 1,900m✦Founded in Nairobi✦Gulf Export Specialists✦ Kenya AA Grade✦Kirinyaga · 1,900m✦Founded in Nairobi✦Gulf Export Specialists✦

"Kenya has always grown the world's finest coffee. We simply decided the world deserves to know exactly where it comes from — and who grew it."

Kibo was founded in Nairobi with a single conviction — that the highlands of Kirinyaga, Nyeri and Murang'a produce coffee that belongs alongside the greatest luxury goods in the world. Not as a commodity. Not as an afterthought. But as the centrepiece of a world-class brand.

For too long, Kenya's AA grade coffee has been exported as a raw material, stripped of its story, blended into anonymity, and sold without the premium it deserves. Kibo was created to change that. Every lot we source is traceable, every farm we work with is visited, and every buyer we supply understands exactly what they are receiving and where it came from.

Our name comes from Kibo — the highest peak of Kilimanjaro, Africa's roof. It represents altitude, excellence, and the conviction that the best things in life are found at the top.

Our Mission

What We Stand For

Four principles guide every decision we make — from the farm we partner with to the contract we sign.

01
Uncompromising Quality
We only source AA and AB grade lots. If a harvest doesn't meet our cupping standard, we don't buy it. No exceptions.
02
Full Traceability
Every lot is traceable to the cooperative, the region, and the harvest date. No blends. No anonymity. Complete transparency from farm to buyer.
03
Farmer Partnership
We pay premium prices directly to cooperatives. We visit every farm we source from. The people who grow Kibo coffee share in its success.
04
Luxury Positioning
Kenya AA is one of the rarest coffees on earth. We market it as such — not as a commodity but as a world-class luxury product deserving world-class buyers.
Our Journey

The Kibo Story

2025

The Idea

Founded in Nairobi with a vision to create the world's most luxurious Kenyan coffee export brand. First farm visits in Kirinyaga, Nyeri and Murang'a.

2026 · Q1

Kibo is Born

kibo.coffee launches. Brand identity, packaging concept and the first three lots — Summit Reserve, Midnight Bloom and Golden Mist — are established.

2026 · Q2

Gulf Market Entry

First export inquiries from UAE and Saudi Arabia. Export infrastructure established with FOB Mombasa shipping routes to all six Gulf markets.

2026 & Beyond

The World Awaits

Private label programme launching for Gulf luxury hotel partners. Expansion into European specialty market planned. The Kibo story is just beginning.
